Accommodations for persons with disabilities

Anyone with a disability who requires assistance in order to participate in the programs, activities or services of the Miami-Dade County Animal Services Department, may call Luis Salgado at 305-418-7162. You can request materials in an accessible format, ask for sign language interpreters, or request other means of equally effective communication to participate in any program or meeting. Contact Luis Salgado at least five days in advance of date of needed services. TTY users may also call 711 (Florida Relay Service.)
